Development process

To make the coordination with the client efficient, we use the following steps:
Vision – Client contacts us and tells his vision.
Meeting – We meet with the client to find out all his wishes in detail. We ask questions and suggest additional ideas. (The meeting is done face to face or via Skype.)
Analysis – We work through all the information we have gathered and consider different solutions for achieving the most effective result.
Coordination – In this stage we coordinate with the client for possible solutions, tell the cost of the project and make an agreement.
Project – We start developing the application.
Testing – After completing the initial solution, we test the application thoroughly.
Presentation – We present the completed and tested application to the client.
Delivering – After receiving positive feedback from the client, we deliver the full-scale solution.
